Streamline requires macOS 12 Monterey or newer.

Save time by typing less

Streamline automatically expands short sequences of characters of your choosing into longer phrases and sentences you use often.

Organize your automations

Streamline lets you organize your text-expanding automation workflows. Create new workflows and organize them into groups. Easily export groups to share them with others.

Search from any app

Text expansion can be triggered by typing the trigger phrase, or by bringing up the global search panel from any app. Type a few characters to find the text you're looking for.
